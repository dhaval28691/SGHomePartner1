Jewel @ Buangkok

Jewel@Buangkok - an ideal mix of fun, wellness and modern interiors designed for family living. Awarded with BCA Green Mark Award with the green features that provide energy and water efficiency, environmental quality and protection and other green innovations. All units oriented along north-south direction to minimize east-west solar gain.

Unique Features of Jewel @ Buangkok

  • 3 minutes’ walk to Buangkok MRT Station
  • Easy access to Orchard Road, Central Business District area and other parts of Singapore.
  • Well connected to the expressways CTE, KPE and TPE
  • Near to upcoming rail network by 2030 at Hougang (50km cross island line (CRL) that will connect to Changi to Jurong Industrial estate
  • Stone throw away to Sengkang Community Hub, Sengkang Sports and Recreation Centre, Sengkang Riverside Park and Punggol Park.
  • Near to upcoming Jalan Kayu sports hub in Buangkok Crescent, which will feature multi purpose sports facility, skating zone & yoga area.
  • Units oriented along north-south direction to minimize east-west solar gain
  • Building with Green Features – awarded with Building Construction Authority Green Mark Award (Gold Plus)
  • Energy efficient air-conditioners provided to all units
  • One unit of Hyflux water filtration system will be installed at every Kitchen
  • Extensive and dense greenery and landscape areas

Passive cool design architecture and façade elements based on the concept of random screens adding a layer of dynamics in the modern context. The screens for select units are designed as sun shading elements at balconies of the living/dining areas or master bedroom, in a matrix forming a vibrant play and bringing character to the units. These vertical screens create a light-friendly and comfortable environment at the balconies as extended living/dining or master bedroom spaces.

6 tower blocks staggered to maximize natural ventilation. Most 616 units oriented along north-south direction to minimize east-west solar gain.
