WingTai Holdings Limited is Singapore’s leading property developer and lifestyle company reputed for quality and design. It has been awarded for its commitment to environmental sustainability, innovative design and architectural conservation. For projects like L’VIV @ Newton, Helios Residence @ Carnhill, Belle Vue Residence @ Oxley Walk, Ascentia Sky @ Alexandra & Foresque Residences.

Why Tembusu ? The Tembusu stands as an enduring symbol of integrity and resilience – a fitting corporate logo for Wing Tai as it sets its vision on steady and conﬁdent growth.

The Tembusu is a large evergreen tree and is noted for its deep roots and extensive branch system. It easily lives over 100 years. Its deeply grained bark conveys character and tenacity.

The current location of The Tembusu condominium used to be the headquarters of Wing Tai Holdings Pte Ltd since its incorporation Singapore in 1963. From retail to hospitality, residential and commercial activities, Wing Tai has grown from strength-to-strength here, with success and growth in all their core business areas .
